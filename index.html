Who We Are

ABOUT

Flavo Kreatives is a Lagos-based creative studio led by Kayode Adeife Obasunlade — a designer, creative director, and visual strategist with a sharp eye for what makes a brand unforgettable.

We work across real estate, fintech, automotive, and lifestyle — building visual systems that are as functional as they are striking. Every project is treated as a brand problem first, a design problem second.

From social content to luxury print campaigns — we do the work that moves brands forward.
