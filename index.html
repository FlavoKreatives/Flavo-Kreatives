Who We Are

ABOUT

Flavo Kreatives started in 2018. By 2020, it became intentional — a deliberate decision to go deep into the craft and build something that actually means something in the Lagos creative space.

The work here is built on one principle: a design that looks good but fails to communicate is not a good design. Every project starts with the message, the audience, and the brief. The aesthetics follow — not the other way around.

We work with brands that understand what design actually does. Not decoration. Not showbiz. Communication with intention.

Working across Photoshop, Illustrator, CorelDraw, Figma, After Effects and Premiere Pro — with AI tools including Midjourney and Leonardo for concept generation.
